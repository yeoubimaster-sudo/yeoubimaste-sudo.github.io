Company Overview

YEOUBI Inc. is a software company based in Daegu, South Korea. We develop mobile applications and digital services focused on real-time information and user decision-making.

Product

Boneun Nalssi (보는날씨)

Boneun Nalssi is a mobile application that allows users to check real-time weather and actual field conditions through live CCTV streams at outdoor sports venues such as baseball fields, futsal courts, football fields, and tennis courts.

The service helps users determine whether conditions are suitable before visiting a location.
